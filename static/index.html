What is LINK?

The LINK conference is an award winning event organized by the Rotaract Club of Milimani to connect young individuals between 18-30 years with leaders from various professions in line with the Rotaract goal of professional and leadership development. LINK also provides this demographic with a value system that inherently advances social development that has continuity and sustainability.

LINK was awarded the Rotary Youth Leadership Award (R.Y.L.A) in 2009/2010 and in 2011/2012.
