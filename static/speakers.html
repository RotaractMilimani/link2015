The Speakers

David Waweru Self Leadership

David Waweru is an entrepreneur, publisher and trainer who is committed to helping individuals and organizations attain their highest levels of performance and productivity. He draws on his own past experiences, on his extensive travels, and on his study, both of human nature and of the subjects he is passionate about to help others achieve their dreams.

David earned his MBA from Curtin Graduate School of Business in Perth, Australia. He has been a Golden Gloves amateur boxing champion, a high school science teacher, a science editor, and a leader on the Boards of several organizations.

Manyara Kirago Money Management

Manyara Kirago is a registered Financial Consultant (RFC) & Chartered Mutual Funds Counselor (CMFC). He is a member of the International Association of Registered Financial Consultants, founder and Managing Director of Financial Counseling Ltd.He is the author of the book “How to Become a Lifelong Financial Success” and has been quoted in numerous Personal Finance articles as a Personal Finance expert. Manyara has personally conducted personal finance training in many blue chip companies and now leads a team of qualified and polished Financial Wellness Counsellors at Financial Counselling Ltd.

Winnie Gor Connecting for Success

Winnie Gor is a Lead PR consultant at Winnie Gor Consultancy. She’s been a PR practitioner for the past 7 years well versed in Brand PR, Corporate Communications, Events, and Strategic media relations. She previously worked at the renowned Gina Din Corporate communications where she handled various corporates including Safaricom, Unilever (OMO, Royco, Blueband and Vaseline Cocoa Butter), General Motors, Diamond Trust Bank, Old Mutual Kenya amongst others and managed conferences like AGOA, AFCAC,COMESA.

She helped set up and launch the Healthy Woman Magazine which is currently the Leading health, fitness and lifestyle magazine in Kenya.Winnie headed the PR and marketing department at Laugh Industry and was in charge of brands like Kids Festival, Theatres and Churchill Show respectively.

She has consulted for Africa Practice as a brand PR consultant during handling L’oreal Kenya where she spearheaded the launch of L’Oreal’s GarniernBB product and Fairtrade East Africa.

She has since handled clients like HP, Rupu, AERC, Women for Cancer, Habitat for Humanity Kenya, Guardian Bank, DMC Group, Expose Kenya and EU respectively.

Danson Muchemi Connecting for Success

Danson Muchemi is the Founder & Chief Executive Officer, JamboPay an electronic payments services provider based Kenya. Danson has vast experience in electronic payments field. He has steered JamboPay into a Top Notch payments processer in Africa. JamboPay processes paymentsfor over 1000 enterprises in Kenya. Notable JamboPay projects includes the multi billion electronic payment system for Kenya's Capital City-Nairobi.

He has spearheaded integration of mobile money systems to 3rd party systems and services including M-PESA, Airtel Money and YuCASH. He has also led VISA, MasterCard and other payments cards integration in various commercial areas. JamboPay and Danson Muchemi represent the rising confidence in Africa’s economic transformation.

At 31, Danson personifies the aspiration of the youth, professionals and entrepreneurs across the African continent. “My goals are to create a global network of payments systems for Africa. A super gateway to and from Africa,” Danson, a graduate of Kenyatta University says.

JamboPay is one of the fastest growing business entreprises in East Africa.

In 2013, JamboPay won the Google Innovation Awards 2013-2014 in the Financial Services for their innovative approach to electronic payments . Danson has been listed by US Forbes Magazine in the Top 30 Most Promising Young Entrepreneurs in Africa – 2014.
